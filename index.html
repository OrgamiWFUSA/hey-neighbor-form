Got questions
We have answers
Do I need to be a member to attend events?
Nope! Most Hey Neighbor events are open to members and non-members alike. Members do get priority access and discounted pricing on select events. Joining is always free.
How much do events cost?
Saturday Park Club is always free. Other events like the Wine Society, Supper Club, and Family Brunch have a small cost to cover the venue and any food or drink. We work hard to keep everything affordable, and members always get the best pricing.
What neighborhoods do you cover?
We currently host events on the Upper East Side, Upper West Side, and Forest Hills. We are always looking to grow into new neighborhoods. If you think your block is ready for a Hey Neighbor community, reach out and let us know.
Can I bring my kids to events?
It depends on the event! Family Brunch and Saturday Park Club are designed for the whole family. Wine Society and the Supper Club are adults only so you can actually have an uninterrupted conversation. Event listings always make this clear upfront.
We are a school or building. How do we work with you?
We love partnering with schools, building management companies, and organizations who want to help their families connect. We handle all the planning and execution so you can focus on your community. Reach out through our contact page and we will set up a call.
What is the community board and how do I get access?
The community board is a private space for Hey Neighbor members to ask questions, share recommendations, and connect with parents in their neighborhood. Join for free to get access.
